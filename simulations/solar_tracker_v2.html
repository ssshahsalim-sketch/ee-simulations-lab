⚡ Inverter Type

String Inverter
Multiple panels in series → one central inverter.
Best for: Large uniform arrays, minimal shading.
Efficiency: 97–98% · Lower cost per Wp
Monitoring: String-level only
🔬
Microinverter
One inverter per panel — independent MPPT.
Best for: Partial shading, complex rooftops.
Efficiency: 95–96% per unit · Higher cost
Monitoring: Panel-level real-time

Best for: Mixed shading, monitoring needs.
Efficiency: 98–99% system · Mid cost
Monitoring: Panel-level DC
