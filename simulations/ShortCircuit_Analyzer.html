Getting Started:
1. Drag components from the left panel onto the canvas.
2. Click a component to select and edit its parameters.
3. Use Wire Mode (W) or drag from port dots to connect.
4. Place a Fault Point (F) on a bus and click Run All Faults.

Fault Types: 3-Phase (LLL), Line-Ground (LG), Line-Line (LL), Line-Line-Ground (LLG).
