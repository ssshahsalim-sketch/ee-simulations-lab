      Note on the physics modelled here: line-side current waveforms are synthesised from typical characteristic harmonic orders (h = 6k±1) of a 6-pulse diode rectifier; actual THD depends on source impedance (SCR/Isc-to-IL ratio), DC bus capacitor/inductor design, and loading. Load-side dV/dt and overshoot figures are illustrative, based on the transmission-line reflection model (Vpeak approaches 2× DC bus for cable length ≥ critical length, Lcrit ≈ v·tr/2, with cable propagation velocity v ≈ 150 m/µs for typical PVC/XLPE power cable). Use manufacturer datasheets and IEEE 519 / NEMA MG1 Part 30-31 calculations for actual filter sizing and compliance work.
